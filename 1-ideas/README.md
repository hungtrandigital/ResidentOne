## Purpose

This directory is for **initial, exploratory work**:
- Market research and analysis
- Early financing ideas
- Initial go-to-market strategies

## Document Lifecycle

- **Initial versions** stay in department folders (e.g., `finance/1.2-initial-financing-plan.md`)
- **Final, polished versions** move to proper sections:
  - `finance/1.2-initial-financing-plan.md` → `5-financing/plans.md`
  - `marketing/1.3-initial-go-to-market-plan.md` → `4-marketing/go-to-market.md`
- When moving documents, leave a Markdown link pointing to the new location

## Organization by Department

Ideas and plans are organized by department for easy navigation:
- **Marketing:** GTM strategies, marketing features, campaigns
- **Technical:** Architecture decisions, technical features, infrastructure
- **Operations:** Process improvements, operational features, automation
- **Finance:** Business models, financing plans, pricing strategies
- **Product:** Product features, UX improvements, product strategy
