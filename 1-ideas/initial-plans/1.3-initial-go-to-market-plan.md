# Initial Go-to-Market Plan

_This is an initial, exploratory go-to-market plan. When finalized, this document should be moved to `4-marketing/go-to-market.md`._

## Overview

This document outlines the initial go-to-market strategy for the building marketplace platform, including customer acquisition channels, pricing strategies, and sales approaches.

**Last Updated:** 2025-12-12

## Customer Acquisition Channels

### Channel 1: Direct Sales to Buildings
**Target:** Individual buildings (BQT/BQL)

**Approach:**
- Direct outreach to building management
- Personal connections and referrals
- Building visits and presentations
- Case studies and testimonials

**Pricing Strategy:**
- Early bird: VND 4M/month (first 50 buildings)
- Standard: VND 8M/month
- Trial: 30-day free trial

**CAC:** VND 2-4M per building
**Conversion Rate:** 20-30%
**Target:** 20-30 buildings in Year 1

### Channel 2: Property Developer Partnerships
**Target:** Property developers (chủ đầu tư)

**Approach:**
- Partner with major developers
- Include platform in new building projects
- Developer-branded platform option
- Revenue share model

**Pricing Strategy:**
- Loss leader: Free first building
- Standard: VND 10-20M per building (one-time)
- Bulk discount: VND 5-10M per building (10+ buildings)
- Revenue share: 20-30% of subscription revenue

**CAC:** VND 250K-2M per building (much lower)
**Conversion Rate:** 80-90% for additional buildings
**Target:** 10-20 developer partnerships, 50-200 buildings

### Channel 3: BQL Company Partnerships
**Target:** BQL companies (công ty quản lý tòa nhà)

**Approach:**
- Partner with major BQL companies
- White-label option for BQL companies
- Bulk licensing
- Revenue share model

**Pricing Strategy:**
- Loss leader: Free first 3 buildings
- Volume discount: VND 3-5M per building/month (20+ buildings)
- Revenue share: 20-30% of subscription revenue
- White-label: Revenue share option

**CAC:** VND 250K-2M per building
**Conversion Rate:** 85-95% for additional buildings
**Target:** 5-10 BQL company partnerships, 100-500 buildings

### Channel 4: Referral Program
**Target:** Existing customers

**Approach:**
- Incentivize existing customers to refer
- Referral rewards for both referrer and referee
- Track and reward referrals

**Pricing Strategy:**
- Referrer reward: 1 month free or VND 5M cash
- Referee reward: 2 months free or 25% discount
- Revenue share: 20% of referred building revenue (first year)

**CAC:** VND 8-16M per building (reward cost)
**Conversion Rate:** 60-80%
**Target:** 20-30% of new buildings from referrals

### Channel 5: Digital Marketing
**Target:** Building management, property developers, BQL companies

**Approach:**
- Content marketing (blog, case studies)
- Social media marketing
- Search engine marketing (SEM)
- Industry events and conferences

**Pricing Strategy:**
- Early bird offers
- Free trial promotions
- Webinar and demo offers

**CAC:** VND 1-3M per building
**Conversion Rate:** 10-20%
**Target:** 10-20 buildings in Year 1

## Pricing Strategy by Channel

### Direct Sales
**Strategy:** Early Bird + Trial
- Early bird: VND 4M/month (first 50 buildings)
- Standard: VND 8M/month
- Trial: 30-day free trial
- **Target:** Fast initial adoption

### Developer Partnerships
**Strategy:** Loss Leader + Revenue Share
- Free first building
- VND 10-20M per building (one-time)
- Revenue share: 20-30%
- **Target:** Multiple buildings per developer

### BQL Company Partnerships
**Strategy:** Volume Discounts + Revenue Share
- Free first 3 buildings
- VND 3-5M per building/month (bulk)
- Revenue share: 20-30%
- **Target:** Bulk acquisition

### Referral Program
**Strategy:** Dual Rewards
- Referrer: 1 month free or VND 5M
- Referee: 2 months free or 25% discount
- **Target:** Viral growth

## Sales Process

### Step 1: Lead Generation
**Methods:**
- Direct outreach (email, phone, in-person)
- Referrals from existing customers
- Partnerships (developers, BQL companies)
- Digital marketing (content, social, SEM)
- Industry events and conferences

**Target:** 200-500 leads per month

### Step 2: Qualification
**Criteria:**
- Building size: 50+ units
- Active trading (Facebook/Zalo groups)
- BQT/BQL structure
- Willingness to pay
- Decision-making authority

**Target:** 50-100 qualified leads per month

### Step 3: Demo & Presentation
**Content:**
- Platform demo
- Value proposition
- Case studies
- Pricing options
- Q&A

**Target:** 20-30 demos per month

### Step 4: Proposal & Pricing
**Options:**
- Standard pricing
- Early bird pricing
- Trial period
- Custom pricing (for large deals)

**Target:** 10-15 proposals per month

### Step 5: Negotiation & Closing
**Approach:**
- Address objections
- Negotiate pricing
- Customize offering
- Close deal

**Target:** 5-10 closed deals per month

### Step 6: Onboarding
**Process:**
- Setup platform
- Training
- Support
- Success metrics

**Target:** 100% onboarding success

## Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC) by Channel

### Direct Sales
- Sales team: VND 50-100M/month
- Marketing: VND 30-60M/month
- Leads: 200-500/month
- Conversions: 20-30/month
- **CAC: VND 2-4M per building**

### Developer Partnerships
- Partner acquisition: VND 5-10M per partner
- Buildings per partner: 5-20 buildings
- **CAC: VND 250K-2M per building**

### BQL Company Partnerships
- Partner acquisition: VND 10-20M per partner
- Buildings per partner: 20-50 buildings
- **CAC: VND 200K-1M per building**

### Referral Program
- Reward cost: VND 8-16M per building
- **CAC: VND 8-16M per building** (but higher conversion)

### Digital Marketing
- Marketing spend: VND 20-50M/month
- Leads: 100-200/month
- Conversions: 10-20/month
- **CAC: VND 1-3M per building**

## First 100 Customers Plan

### Phase 1: First 10 Customers (Months 1-2)
**Strategy:** Loss Leader + Personal Connections
- Free first 3 months
- Personal connections and referrals
- High-touch onboarding
- Case study development

**Target:** 10 buildings
**CAC:** VND 30-40M per building (loss leader)
**Revenue:** VND 0 (free period)

### Phase 2: Next 20 Customers (Months 3-4)
**Strategy:** Early Bird + Referrals
- Early bird pricing: VND 4M/month
- Referral program launch
- Case studies and testimonials
- Developer partnerships (first 2-3)

**Target:** 30 buildings total
**CAC:** VND 2-4M per building
**Revenue:** VND 80-120M/month

### Phase 3: Next 30 Customers (Months 5-6)
**Strategy:** Freemium + Partnerships
- Freemium model launch
- BQL company partnerships (first 2-3)
- Developer partnerships expansion
- Referral program growth

**Target:** 60 buildings total
**CAC:** VND 1-3M per building
**Revenue:** VND 300-450M/month

### Phase 4: Next 40 Customers (Months 7-9)
**Strategy:** Scale + Optimization
- All channels active
- Pricing optimization
- Conversion optimization
- Partnership expansion

**Target:** 100 buildings total
**CAC:** VND 1-2M per building
**Revenue:** VND 600-800M/month

## Key Success Metrics

### Acquisition Metrics
- **Leads Generated:** 200-500 per month
- **Qualified Leads:** 50-100 per month
- **Demos:** 20-30 per month
- **Proposals:** 10-15 per month
- **Closed Deals:** 5-10 per month
- **CAC:** VND 1-4M per building

### Conversion Metrics
- **Lead to Qualified:** 20-25%
- **Qualified to Demo:** 40-60%
- **Demo to Proposal:** 50-70%
- **Proposal to Close:** 50-70%
- **Overall Conversion:** 2-5%

### Revenue Metrics
- **Revenue per Building:** VND 6-10M/month
- **Monthly Recurring Revenue (MRR):** VND 300M-1B/month (Year 1)
- **Annual Recurring Revenue (ARR):** VND 3.6B-12B/year (Year 1)

### Retention Metrics
- **Churn Rate:** <5% per month
- **Retention Rate:** >95% per month
- **LTV:** VND 168M per building
