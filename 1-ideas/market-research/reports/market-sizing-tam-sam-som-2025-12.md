# Market Sizing - TAM/SAM/SOM Analysis

**Date:** 2025-12-12  
**Last Updated:** 2025-12-12  
**Research Type:** Market Sizing Analysis  
**Focus:** Calculate Total Addressable Market (TAM), Serviceable Addressable Market (SAM), and Serviceable Obtainable Market (SOM) for building marketplace platform

## Executive Summary

This report calculates the market size for the building marketplace platform in Vietnam, focusing on HCMC and Hanoi as initial target markets. The analysis includes TAM (all buildings in Vietnam), SAM (buildings in HCMC/Hanoi), and SOM (initial target buildings).

**Key Findings:**
- **TAM:** ~3,100+ buildings in Vietnam (conservative estimate)
- **SAM:** ~3,100 buildings in HCMC and Hanoi (primary target)
- **SOM (Year 1):** 5-10 buildings (pilot phase)
- **SOM (Year 3):** 50-100 buildings (scaled phase)

## Market Size Calculations

### Total Addressable Market (TAM)

**Definition:** Total number of residential buildings in Vietnam that could potentially use the platform.

**Calculation:**
- **Hà Nội:** ~1,579 old buildings (confirmed) + new buildings (estimated 500-1,000) = **~2,000-2,500 buildings**
- **TP.HCM:** ~1,521 old buildings (confirmed) + new buildings (estimated 500-1,000) = **~2,000-2,500 buildings**
- **Other Major Cities:** Đà Nẵng, Cần Thơ, Hải Phòng (estimated 200-500 buildings total)

**TAM Estimate:** **~4,200-5,500 buildings** (conservative: **~3,100 buildings**)

**Assumptions:**
- Only residential buildings (chung cư) with active communities
- Buildings with 50+ units (minimum viable size)
- Buildings with active trading (Facebook/Zalo groups)

### Serviceable Addressable Market (SAM)

**Definition:** Number of buildings in HCMC and Hanoi that are suitable for the platform.

**Calculation:**
- **Hà Nội:** ~2,000-2,500 buildings
- **TP.HCM:** ~2,000-2,500 buildings

**SAM Estimate:** **~4,000-5,000 buildings** (conservative: **~3,100 buildings**)

**Target Criteria:**
- Buildings in HCMC and Hanoi (initial focus)
- Buildings with active trading communities (Facebook/Zalo groups)
- Buildings with 50+ units (minimum viable size)
- Buildings with BQT/BQL management structure
- Buildings with resident engagement (information sharing, buying/selling)

**Market Segmentation:**
1. **High-Priority Buildings (20%):** 600-1,000 buildings
   - Large buildings (200+ units)
   - Active trading communities
   - High resident engagement
   - BQT/BQL turnover issues

2. **Medium-Priority Buildings (50%):** 1,500-2,500 buildings
   - Medium buildings (100-200 units)
   - Moderate trading activity
   - Some resident engagement

3. **Low-Priority Buildings (30%):** 900-1,500 buildings
   - Small buildings (50-100 units)
   - Limited trading activity
   - Low resident engagement

### Serviceable Obtainable Market (SOM)

**Definition:** Number of buildings we can realistically acquire and serve in the first 1-3 years.

#### Year 1 (Pilot Phase)
**Target:** 5-10 buildings

**Strategy:**
- Focus on high-priority buildings
- Personal connections and referrals
- Proof of concept validation
- Limited marketing budget

**Success Criteria:**
- 5-10 active buildings
- 50-200 active users per building
- 20-50 active sellers per building
- Positive user feedback

#### Year 2 (Growth Phase)
**Target:** 20-50 buildings

**Strategy:**
- Expand to medium-priority buildings
- Referral program
- Partnership with property developers
- Increased marketing budget

**Success Criteria:**
- 20-50 active buildings
- 100-500 active users per building
- 50-200 active sellers per building
- Revenue: VND 100-500M/month

#### Year 3 (Scale Phase)
**Target:** 50-100 buildings

**Strategy:**
- Scale across HCMC and Hanoi
- Brand recognition ("Chợ chung cư" brand)
- Partnership expansion
- Optimized acquisition process

**Success Criteria:**
- 50-100 active buildings
- 200-1,000 active users per building
- 100-500 active sellers per building
- Revenue: VND 425M-1.8B/month

**SOM Estimates:**
- **Year 1:** 5-10 buildings (0.2-0.3% of SAM)
- **Year 2:** 20-50 buildings (0.6-1.6% of SAM)
- **Year 3:** 50-100 buildings (1.6-3.2% of SAM)

## Market Size by Industry

### Retail Market (Bán Lẻ)

**Target:** Housewives selling products to building residents

**Market Size:**
- **Buildings:** All SAM buildings (3,100 buildings)
- **Potential Sellers:** 5-10% of residents = 50-200 sellers per building
- **Total Potential Sellers:** 155,000-620,000 sellers (across all buildings)
- **Active Sellers (Year 3):** 5,000-50,000 sellers (50-100 buildings × 50-500 sellers)

**Revenue Potential:**
- Building subscriptions: VND 5-10M/month per building
- Seller subscriptions: VND 200K-500K/month per seller (future)
- Total (Year 3): VND 250M-1B/month from building subscriptions

### Real Estate Market (Môi Giới Nhà Đất)

**Target:** Property owners self-listing, verified real estate agents

**Market Size:**
- **Buildings:** All SAM buildings (3,100 buildings)
- **Potential Listings:** 1-5% of units = 1-10 listings per building per year
- **Total Potential Listings:** 3,100-31,000 listings per year
- **Active Listings (Year 3):** 50-1,000 listings per year (50-100 buildings)

**Revenue Potential:**
- Listing fees: VND 1-2M per listing (vs. 1-2% brokerage fee = VND 50-200M saved)
- Agent subscriptions: VND 2-5M/month per agent
- Total (Year 3): VND 50M-2B/month from listings and subscriptions

**Market Opportunity:**
- Current brokerage fees: 1-2% of property value (VND 50M-200M per transaction)
- Platform listing fee: VND 1-2M per listing (99% cost savings for owners)
- Value proposition: Owners save VND 48M-198M per transaction

### Services Market (Dịch Vụ)

**Target:** Local businesses (phở shops, gyms, restaurants, maintenance, cleaning, etc.)

**Market Size:**
- **Buildings:** All SAM buildings (3,100 buildings)
- **Potential Businesses:** 5-20 businesses per building (nearby)
- **Total Potential Businesses:** 15,500-62,000 businesses
- **Active Businesses (Year 3):** 250-2,000 businesses (50-100 buildings × 5-20 businesses)

**Revenue Potential:**
- Business subscriptions: VND 2-5M/month per business
- Total (Year 3): VND 500M-10B/month from business subscriptions

## Revenue Projections

### Year 1 (5-10 buildings)
- **Building Subscriptions:** 5-10 buildings × VND 5-10M/month = **VND 25-100M/month**
- **Business Subscriptions:** 25-100 businesses × VND 2-5M/month = **VND 50-500M/month**
- **Total Revenue:** **VND 75-600M/month** (VND 900M-7.2B/year)

### Year 2 (20-50 buildings)
- **Building Subscriptions:** 20-50 buildings × VND 5-10M/month = **VND 100-500M/month**
- **Business Subscriptions:** 100-500 businesses × VND 2-5M/month = **VND 200-2.5B/month**
- **Listing Fees:** 20-100 listings × VND 1-2M = **VND 20-200M/month**
- **Total Revenue:** **VND 320-3.2B/month** (VND 3.84B-38.4B/year)

### Year 3 (50-100 buildings)
- **Building Subscriptions:** 50-100 buildings × VND 5-10M/month = **VND 250M-1B/month**
- **Business Subscriptions:** 250-2,000 businesses × VND 2-5M/month = **VND 500M-10B/month**
- **Listing Fees:** 50-1,000 listings × VND 1-2M = **VND 50M-2B/month**
- **Total Revenue:** **VND 800M-13B/month** (VND 9.6B-156B/year)
