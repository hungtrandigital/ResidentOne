## 12. Risk Factors & Mitigation

### 12.1 Financial Risks

**Risk:** Revenue slower than projected
- **Mitigation:** Buffer in funding (VND 70M), focus on high-quality channels
- **Impact:** Extend break-even timeline

**Risk:** Costs higher than projected
- **Mitigation:** Leverage existing resources, optimize costs continuously
- **Impact:** Increase funding needs

**Risk:** Higher CAC than projected
- **Mitigation:** Focus on lower-CAC channels (partners, ambassadors)
- **Impact:** Reduce growth, extend break-even

### 12.2 Operational Risks

**Risk:** Higher churn than projected
- **Mitigation:** Improve retention, optimize onboarding
- **Impact:** Reduce LTV, extend break-even

**Risk:** Slower building growth
- **Mitigation:** Optimize sales process, focus on high-quality channels
- **Impact:** Reduce revenue, extend break-even

**Risk:** Team capacity constraints
- **Mitigation:** Leverage existing resources, phased hiring
- **Impact:** Slow growth, extend break-even

### 12.3 Market Risks

**Risk:** Competitive pressure
- **Mitigation:** First-mover advantage, network effects, resident ownership
- **Impact:** Reduce pricing, increase CAC

**Risk:** Market adoption slower
- **Mitigation:** Community ambassador model, strong value proposition
- **Impact:** Reduce growth, extend break-even
