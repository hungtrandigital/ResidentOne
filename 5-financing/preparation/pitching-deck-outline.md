## Design Guidelines

### Visual Style
- **Color Scheme:** Professional, modern (blues, greens, whites)
- **Typography:** Clean, readable (San Francisco, Inter, or similar)
- **Imagery:** Real building/community photos, product mockups
- **Charts:** Clear, data-driven, easy to understand

### Presentation Tips
- **Keep it concise:** 10-15 slides max
- **Tell a story:** Problem ‚Üí Solution ‚Üí Market ‚Üí Team ‚Üí Ask
- **Use data:** Numbers, metrics, projections
- **Show traction:** MVP results, milestones
- **Be specific:** Clear ask, specific use of funds
