## 2. Initial Investment Recommendations

### 2.1 Recommended Investment Amount

**Based on Financing Plan Analysis:**

| Scenario | Amount | Runway | Use Case |
|----------|--------|--------|----------|
| **Minimum** | VND 200M | 12 months | Tight, but feasible with leverage existing resources |
| **Recommended** | VND 500M | 12-18 months | Comfortable, buffer for unexpected costs |
| **Ideal** | VND 1B | 18-24 months | Allows faster growth, less pressure |

**Recommendation: VND 500M-1B**

### 2.2 Investment Split Options

#### Option 1: Equal Investment (Recommended)
- **Each Founder:** VND 167M-333M (VND 500M-1B / 3)
- **Pros:** 
  - Equal commitment
  - Simpler structure
  - Fair distribution
- **Cons:** 
  - May not reflect different financial capacities

#### Option 2: Proportional to Equity
- **Hưng:** 50-55% of investment (VND 250M-550M)
- **Tuyền:** 22-25% of investment (VND 110M-250M)
- **Yad:** 22-25% of investment (VND 110M-250M)
- **Pros:** Aligns with equity distribution
- **Cons:** May create unequal financial burden

**Recommendation: Option 1 (Equal Investment) for simplicity**

### 2.3 Use of Funds Breakdown (VND 500M Example)

**Year 1 Allocation:**
- Infrastructure (core hub): VND 45M (9%)
- Variable costs (9 buildings average): VND 231M (46%)
- Team costs (if needed): VND 0-100M (0-20%)
- Loss leader (first 3 months): VND 144M (29%)
- Buffer (unexpected costs): VND 80M (16%)

**Total: VND 500M**

## 3. Pitching Deck Structure

### 3.1 Core Slides (10-15 Slides)

1. **Title Slide** - Company, tagline, presenters
2. **The Problem** - Pain points, market gaps
3. **Market Opportunity** - Size, growth, segments
4. **Our Solution** - Product, features, approach
5. **MVP Validation** - Results, learnings, next steps
6. **Business Model** - Revenue, pricing, unit economics
7. **Go-to-Market** - Strategy, channels, timeline
8. **Competitive Advantage** - Differentiators, moats
9. **Traction & Milestones** - Progress, goals
10. **Team** - Founders, expertise, resources
11. **Financial Projections** - Revenue, costs, profitability
12. **Funding Ask** - Amount, use, milestones
13. **Risks & Mitigation** - Key risks, solutions
14. **Vision & Long-Term** - 5-year goals, expansion
15. **Call to Action** - Contact, next steps

### 3.2 Key Messages

- **Problem:** Critical pain points for residents and building management
- **Solution:** Comprehensive resident-owned platform
- **Validation:** MVP proven at 1 building (60 sellers, 200-300 users)
- **Market:** Large, growing market (~3,000 buildings, ~6.4M users)
- **Team:** Experienced founders with existing resources
- **Ask:** VND 500M-1B for 12-24 month runway

## 4. Founder Agreement Key Terms

### 4.1 Essential Clauses

1. **Equity Distribution** - Exact percentages, vesting
2. **Roles & Responsibilities** - Clear role definitions
3. **Capital Investment** - Amounts, payment schedule
4. **Resource Contributions** - WEBASEGROUP & 8Agency resources
5. **Decision Making** - Board composition, voting rights
6. **Exit Provisions** - Right of first refusal, drag-along, tag-along
7. **Non-Compete & Confidentiality** - Terms, duration
8. **IP Assignment** - Company IP, pre-existing IP
9. **Dispute Resolution** - Mediation, arbitration
10. **Termination** - Voluntary, for cause, without cause

### 4.2 Critical Decisions Needed

1. **Equity Percentages:** Finalize exact percentages
2. **Investment Amounts:** Agree on per-founder investment
3. **Resource Compensation:** How to compensate WEBASEGROUP & 8Agency resources
4. **Board Composition:** Who sits on board, voting structure
5. **Vesting Terms:** Confirm 4-year vesting with 1-year cliff
