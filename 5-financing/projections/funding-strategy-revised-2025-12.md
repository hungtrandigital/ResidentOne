**Strategy:** Use **Realistic Scenario** as base case, with Conservative and Optimistic as sensitivity analysis.

## 6. Risk Mitigation

### 6.1 Funding Risks

**Risk:** Cannot secure external funding
- **Mitigation:** Founder contributions (VND 500M-1B)
- **Impact:** Slower growth, longer runway

**Risk:** Revenue slower than projected
- **Mitigation:** Buffer in funding (VND 200M-500M)
- **Impact:** Extend runway, adjust growth

**Risk:** Costs higher than projected
- **Mitigation:** Leverage existing resources, optimize costs
- **Impact:** Increase funding needs

### 6.2 Growth Risks

**Risk:** Slower building growth
- **Mitigation:** Focus on high-quality channels, optimize sales
- **Impact:** Reduce revenue, extend break-even

**Risk:** Higher churn than projected
- **Mitigation:** Improve retention, optimize onboarding
- **Impact:** Reduce LTV, extend break-even

**Risk:** Higher CAC than projected
- **Mitigation:** Focus on lower-CAC channels, optimize sales
- **Impact:** Increase funding needs, reduce growth
