## Executive Summary

This document provides a **consolidated key metrics dashboard** for investor presentations and internal tracking. The dashboard includes SaaS metrics, growth metrics, efficiency metrics, profitability metrics, and unit economics.

**Dashboard Structure:**
1. **SaaS Metrics** - MRR, ARR, ARR Growth, NRR, GRR
2. **Growth Metrics** - Customer Growth, Revenue Growth, Market Share
3. **Efficiency Metrics** - Magic Number, CAC Ratio, Efficiency Score

6. **Trends** - Year 1, Year 2, Year 3 projections
