## Executive Summary

This document outlines the **validation framework** for testing financial assumptions with pilot buildings. The plan tracks key metrics (CAC, churn, conversion) to validate and adjust financial projections based on real data.

**Key Metrics to Validate:**
- **CAC (Customer Acquisition Cost):** VND 8-15M projected vs actual
- **Churn Rate:** 3-4% monthly projected vs actual
- **Conversion Rate:** 40-50% free to paid projected vs actual
- **LTV (Lifetime Value):** VND 100M projected vs actual
- **Building Growth:** 3-5 buildings/month projected vs actual

**Validation Timeline:**
- **Month 3-6:** Pilot phase (5-10 buildings)
- **Month 7-12:** Growth phase (20-35 buildings)
- **Month 13-18:** Scale phase (40-60 buildings)

## 2. CAC Validation

### 2.1 CAC Calculation

**Direct Sales CAC:**
```
CAC = (Sales Team Costs + Marketing Costs) / Buildings Acquired
```

**Components to Track:**
- Sales team salaries: VND 15-25M/month per person
- Sales commissions: VND 2-5M per building
- Marketing spend: VND 5-10M/month
- Sales overhead: VND 2-3M/month

**Expected CAC:** VND 8-15M per building

**Validation Criteria:**
- ✅ **On Target:** VND 8-15M
- ⚠️ **High:** VND 15-20M (needs optimization)
- ❌ **Very High:** >VND 20M (critical issue)

### 2.2 CAC by Channel

**Partner Sales CAC:**
- Expected: VND 2-5M per building
- Track: Partner acquisition cost, buildings per partner, support costs

**Community Ambassador CAC:**
- Expected: VND 1-2M per building
- Track: Commission costs, support costs, buildings per ambassador

**Loss Leader CAC:**
- Expected: VND 40-60M per building
- Track: Free period cost, conversion rate, total CAC

### 2.3 CAC Adjustment Criteria

**If CAC is Higher than Projected:**
- **Action 1:** Focus on lower-CAC channels (partners, ambassadors)
- **Action 2:** Optimize sales process (reduce sales cycle, improve conversion)
- **Action 3:** Reduce marketing spend (focus on high-ROI channels)
- **Impact:** Reduce growth projections, increase funding needs

**If CAC is Lower than Projected:**
- **Action 1:** Scale successful channels
- **Action 2:** Increase growth projections
- **Action 3:** Reduce funding needs
- **Impact:** Faster growth, lower funding needs

## 3. Churn Rate Validation

### 3.1 Churn Calculation

**Monthly Churn Rate:**
```
Churn Rate = (Buildings Lost in Month / Buildings at Start of Month) × 100%
```

**Annual Churn Rate:**
```
Annual Churn = 1 - (1 - Monthly Churn)^12
```

**Expected Churn:**
- **Conservative:** 5% monthly (60% annual)
- **Realistic:** 3-4% monthly (36-48% annual)
- **Optimistic:** 2-3% monthly (24-36% annual)

### 3.2 Churn Analysis

**Churn by Reason:**
- BQT/BQL change (platform independence not valued)
- Pricing too high
- Low value/engagement
- Technical issues
- Competition (Facebook/Zalo)
- Other reasons

**Churn by Segment:**
- Social housing (Nhà ở Xã hội)
- Affordable (Bình dân)
- Mid-range (Trung cấp)
- High-end (Cao cấp)

**Churn by Channel:**
- Direct sales
- Partner sales
- Community ambassador
- Loss leader

### 3.3 Churn Adjustment Criteria

**If Churn is Higher than Projected:**
- **Action 1:** Improve onboarding process
- **Action 2:** Increase retention efforts (customer success, support)
- **Action 3:** Adjust pricing/value proposition
- **Impact:** Reduce LTV, adjust break-even timeline

**If Churn is Lower than Projected:**
- **Action 1:** Increase LTV projections
- **Action 2:** Adjust break-even timeline (faster)
- **Action 3:** Scale retention strategies
- **Impact:** Higher LTV, faster profitability

## 4. Conversion Rate Validation

**Free to Paid Conversion:**
```
Conversion Rate = (Paid Buildings / Total Buildings) × 100%
```

**Expected Conversion:**

- **Realistic:** 40-50%
- **Optimistic:** 50-60%

**Trial to Paid Conversion:**
- Expected: 40-60% (after 3-month free trial)

**Lead to Customer Conversion:**
- Expected: 10-20% (from qualified leads)

### 4.2 Conversion Analysis

**Conversion by Channel:**
- Direct sales: 10-20% (lead to customer)
- Partner sales: 15-25% (lead to customer)
- Community ambassador: 20-30% (lead to customer)

**Conversion by Segment:**

- Mid-range: Higher conversion (target segment)
- High-end: Higher conversion (value-focused)

### 4.3 Conversion Adjustment Criteria

**If Conversion is Lower than Projected:**
- **Action 1:** Improve freemium value proposition
- **Action 2:** Optimize onboarding process
- **Action 3:** Adjust pricing strategy
- **Impact:** Reduce revenue projections, adjust break-even

**If Conversion is Higher than Projected:**
- **Action 1:** Scale successful channels
- **Action 2:** Increase revenue projections
- **Action 3:** Optimize conversion strategies
- **Impact:** Higher revenue, faster profitability

## 5. LTV Validation

### 5.1 LTV Calculation

**LTV Formula:**
```
LTV = (Monthly Revenue × Gross Margin × Average Lifetime)
```

**Components to Track:**
- Monthly revenue: VND 8M/building (average)
- Gross margin: 52% (VND 4.15M/building/month)
- Average lifetime: Based on actual churn rate

**Expected LTV:**
- **Conservative:** VND 80M (20 months × VND 4.15M)
- **Realistic:** VND 100M (28 months × VND 4.15M)
- **Optimistic:** VND 120M (40 months × VND 4.15M)

### 5.2 LTV Analysis

**LTV by Segment:**
- Social housing: Lower LTV (lower pricing, higher churn)
- Affordable: Moderate LTV
- Mid-range: Higher LTV (target segment)
- High-end: Higher LTV (premium pricing)

**LTV by Channel:**
- Direct sales: Standard LTV
- Partner sales: Standard LTV
- Community ambassador: Standard LTV
- Loss leader: Standard LTV (after conversion)

### 5.3 LTV Adjustment Criteria

**If LTV is Lower than Projected:**
- **Action 1:** Improve retention (reduce churn)
- **Action 2:** Increase pricing (if value supports)
- **Action 3:** Adjust unit economics
- **Impact:** Reduce LTV/CAC ratio, adjust profitability

**If LTV is Higher than Projected:**
- **Action 1:** Increase LTV/CAC ratio
- **Action 2:** Adjust profitability projections
- **Action 3:** Scale retention strategies
- **Impact:** Better unit economics, faster profitability

## 9. Success Criteria

### 9.1 Validation Success Metrics

**CAC Validation:**
- ✅ Actual CAC within 20% of projected (VND 8-15M)
- ⚠️ Actual CAC 20-50% higher (needs optimization)
- ❌ Actual CAC >50% higher (critical issue)

**Churn Validation:**
- ✅ Actual churn within 20% of projected (3-4% monthly)
- ⚠️ Actual churn 20-50% higher (needs improvement)
- ❌ Actual churn >50% higher (critical issue)

**Conversion Validation:**
- ✅ Actual conversion within 20% of projected (40-50%)
- ⚠️ Actual conversion 20-50% lower (needs improvement)
- ❌ Actual conversion >50% lower (critical issue)

**LTV Validation:**
- ✅ Actual LTV within 20% of projected (VND 80-120M)
- ⚠️ Actual LTV 20-50% lower (needs improvement)
- ❌ Actual LTV >50% lower (critical issue)

### 9.2 Overall Validation Success

**Success Criteria:**
- ✅ All primary metrics within 20% of projected
- ✅ Unit economics hold at scale
- ✅ Path to profitability is clear
- ✅ Funding strategy is validated

**If Validation Fails:**
- Identify root causes
- Develop mitigation plans
- Adjust projections
- Update funding strategy
- Communicate changes to stakeholders
