## Executive Summary

This document provides **risk-adjusted financial projections** by assigning probability weights to different scenarios and calculating expected values. This approach accounts for uncertainty and provides investors with a more realistic view of potential outcomes.

**Methodology:**
- **Scenario Probabilities:** Conservative (20%), Realistic (60%), Optimistic (20%)
- **Expected Value:** Probability-weighted average of scenarios
- **Downside Case:** Worst-case scenario (10% probability) for risk assessment
- **Risk-Adjusted Break-Even:** Expected timeline to profitability

**Key Outputs:**
- Expected Revenue (Year 1-3)
- Expected Costs (Year 1-3)
- Expected Net Profit/Loss (Year 1-3)
- Expected Break-Even Timeline
- Expected Funding Requirements
- Risk-Adjusted Unit Economics

## 1. Risk-Adjusted Methodology

### 1.1 Scenario Probabilities

**Probability Distribution:**
- **Conservative Scenario:** 20% probability
  - Lower growth, higher costs, higher churn
  - Represents downside risk
- **Realistic Scenario:** 60% probability
  - Based on industry benchmarks and market research
  - Most likely outcome
- **Optimistic Scenario:** 20% probability
  - Higher growth, lower costs, lower churn
  - Represents upside potential
- **Downside Case:** 10% probability (separate from Conservative)
  - Worst-case scenario (all negative variables at +20%)
  - Used for risk assessment and contingency planning

**Rationale:**
- 60% for Realistic reflects most likely outcome
- 20% each for Conservative and Optimistic reflects balanced risk/upside
- 10% for Downside Case reflects extreme risk scenario
- Based on industry best practices for scenario planning

### 1.2 Expected Value Calculation

**Formula:**
Expected Value = (Conservative × 20%) + (Realistic × 60%) + (Optimistic × 20%)

**Example (Year 1 Revenue):**
- Conservative: VND 680M (20%)
- Realistic: VND 1.02B (60%)
- Optimistic: VND 1.36B (20%)
- **Expected Value:** (VND 680M × 20%) + (VND 1.02B × 60%) + (VND 1.36B × 20%) = **VND 1.02B**

### 1.3 Risk-Adjusted Metrics

**Metrics Calculated:**
- Revenue (Year 1-3)
- Costs (Year 1-3)
- Net Profit/Loss (Year 1-3)
- Break-Even Timeline
- Funding Requirements
- Unit Economics (CAC, LTV, LTV/CAC)
