**Recommendation:**
- **Add Sensitivity Analysis Section:**

  - Churn: ±20% impact on LTV and profitability
  - Growth: ±20% impact on revenue and break-even
  - Pricing: ±20% impact on revenue and margins
  - Break-even: Sensitivity to key variables

**Example Format:**
```
Sensitivity Analysis (Realistic Scenario):

Variable: CAC
- Base: VND 10M
- +20%: VND 12M → Impact: -15% profitability, +2 months break-even
- -20%: VND 8M → Impact: +12% profitability, -1.5 months break-even

Variable: Churn Rate
- Base: 3.5% monthly
- +20%: 4.2% monthly → Impact: -18% LTV, +3 months break-even
- -20%: 2.8% monthly → Impact: +15% LTV, -2 months break-even
```
