## 6. Decision Making

### 6.1 Board of Directors

**Composition:**
- **Hưng:** 1 seat (CEO)
- **Tuyền:** 1 seat (Co-Founder)
- **Yad:** 1 seat (Co-Founder)
- **Investor Representative:** 1 seat (if investor joins)
- **Independent Director:** 1 seat (optional)

**Voting:**
- Simple majority for routine decisions
- Supermajority (2/3) for major decisions

### 6.2 Major Decisions Requiring Approval

**Require Board Approval:**
- Raising capital (>VND 500M)
- Major expenses (>VND 100M)
- Hiring key executives
- Strategic partnerships (>VND 50M value)
- Acquisition/merger
- Company sale
- Equity issuance
- Dividend distribution

**Require Founder Unanimous Approval:**
- Change in company structure
- Change in equity distribution
- Change in founder roles
- Dissolution of company

## 7. Exit Provisions

### 7.1 Right of First Refusal

**If Founder Wants to Sell:**
- Must offer to other founders first
- Same terms as third-party offer
- 30-day response period

### 7.2 Drag-Along Rights

**Majority Founders Can:**
- Force minority to sell in acquisition
- Applies if >50% of founders agree
- Same terms and conditions

### 7.3 Tag-Along Rights

**Minority Founders Can:**
- Join majority in sale
- Same terms and conditions
- Protects minority interests

### 7.4 Buyout Provisions

**If Founder Leaves:**
- Company has right to buy back unvested shares
- Vested shares can be sold (subject to right of first refusal)
- Valuation method: [Fair market value / Formula]
