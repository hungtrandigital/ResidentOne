# Team Structure Analysis

**Date:** 2025-12-12  
**Last Updated:** 2025-12-12  
**Status:** Initial Analysis - Resource Mapping

## Purpose

This document analyzes the required team structure for ResidentOne, maps available resources from existing companies (WEBASEGROUP tech company and 8Agency), and identifies positions that need to be filled (either through hiring or equity-based invitations).

**Key Assumptions:**
- Founders/Co-founders work part-time (have own companies)
- Can leverage existing company resources (employees, tools)
- AI coding tools already available (no additional cost)
- Focus on minimizing cash burn in initial phase

**Team Specializations:**
- **Yad (8Agency):** Finance & Strategy (financial planning, business strategy)
- **Tuyền (8Agency):** Marketing Strategy (marketing strategy, marketing planning)
- **8Agency Team:** Market research, marketing planning, content, design
- **WEBASEGROUP Team:** Tech support (backend, frontend, DevOps, QA, junior operations if needed)

## 1. Required Team Structure by Phase

### 1.1 Phase 1: Foundation (Months 1-6)
**Goal:** MVP development, 5-10 pilot buildings

**Required Roles:**

#### Technical Team
1. **Technical Lead / Architect**
   - Responsibilities: Architecture design, code review, process review, technical decisions
   - Skills: System architecture, microservices, Nest.js, PostgreSQL, Redis, Docker
   - Time: Part-time to full-time (depending on phase)

2. **Backend Developer(s)**
   - Responsibilities: Service development, API implementation, database design
   - Skills: Nest.js, TypeScript, PostgreSQL, Redis, REST API
   - Time: Full-time or part-time (depending on AI Factory efficiency)

3. **Frontend Developer(s)**
   - Responsibilities: Mobile-first web app, responsive design, user experience
   - Skills: React/Next.js, TypeScript, mobile-first design, responsive UI
   - Time: Full-time or part-time

4. **DevOps / Infrastructure**
   - Responsibilities: VPS setup, Docker Compose, monitoring, deployment, backups
   - Skills: Linux, Docker, Nginx, monitoring (Prometheus/Grafana), backup strategies
   - Time: Part-time (setup + ongoing maintenance)

5. **QA / Testing**
   - Responsibilities: Testing, bug reporting, quality assurance
   - Skills: Manual testing, test case creation, bug tracking
   - Time: Part-time (can be shared with development team initially)

#### Product & Strategy Team
6. **Product Lead / Founder**
   - Responsibilities: Vision, product strategy, feature prioritization, stakeholder management
   - Skills: Product management, strategic thinking, market understanding
   - Time: Part-time (has own company)

7. **Finance & Strategy Lead**
   - Responsibilities: Financial planning, business strategy, strategic planning, financial analysis
   - Skills: Financial planning, business strategy, strategic analysis, financial modeling
   - Time: Part-time (has own agency)
   - Person: Yad (Co-Founder)

8. **Marketing Strategy Lead**
   - Responsibilities: Marketing strategy, GTM planning, brand positioning, marketing planning
   - Skills: Marketing strategy, brand development, marketing planning, campaign strategy
   - Time: Part-time (has own agency)
   - Person: Tuyền (Co-Founder)

9. **Marketing Execution**
   - Responsibilities: Content creation, social media, community building, market research
   - Skills: Content creation, social media management, community engagement, market research
   - Time: Part-time (can leverage 8Agency team resources)

#### Operations Team
9. **Building Onboarding Specialist**
   - Responsibilities: Building acquisition, onboarding process, customer success
   - Skills: Sales, customer success, relationship building
   - Time: Part-time initially, full-time when scaling

10. **Customer Support**
    - Responsibilities: User support, issue resolution, feedback collection
    - Skills: Customer service, communication, problem-solving

## 5. Equity-Based Invitation Strategy

### 5.1 Positions Suitable for Equity-Only Invitation

**Early Stage (Phase 1-2):**
- Building Onboarding Specialist (if experienced, can work part-time)
- Customer Support Lead (if experienced, can work part-time)
- Sales Lead (if experienced, can work part-time initially)
- Additional Developers (if experienced, can work part-time)

**Rationale:**
- Early stage = higher equity value potential
- Part-time work = lower cash burn
- Experienced people = can work part-time effectively

### 5.2 Positions Requiring Salary

**When Scaling (Phase 2-3):**
- Full-time Sales Team (need guaranteed income)
- Full-time Onboarding Team (need guaranteed income)
- Operations Manager (need guaranteed income)
- Junior Developers (if needed, need guaranteed income)

**Rationale:**
- Full-time commitment = need guaranteed income
- Scaling phase = need reliable, dedicated team
- Junior positions = need salary to attract talent

### 5.3 Hybrid Approach (Equity + Salary)

**For Key Positions:**
- Technical Lead (Ngân): Can be equity + reduced salary (if from ResidentOne)
- Operations Manager: Equity + salary (attract experienced talent)
- Sales Lead: Equity + base salary + commission (align incentives)

## 7. Recommendations

### 7.1 Phase 1 Strategy (Minimal Cash Burn)

**Recommended Approach:**
1. **Leverage Existing Resources:**
   - Use AI Factory (tools available, NO COST)
   - Leverage WEBASEGROUP tech team part-time or full-time (backend, frontend, DevOps, QA, junior operations if needed)
   - Leverage 8Agency team for market research, marketing planning, content, design
   - Technical Lead (Ngân) can be paid by WEBASEGROUP or equity-only
   - Finance & Strategy (Yad) handles financial planning and business strategy
   - Marketing Strategy (Tuyền) handles marketing strategy and planning

2. **Equity-Based Invitations:**
   - Building Onboarding Specialist: Invite experienced person with equity
   - Customer Support: Can be shared with onboarding initially, or equity-based invitation

3. **Minimal Hiring:**
   - Only hire if absolutely necessary (e.g., if AI Factory + part-time support insufficient)
   - Prefer part-time positions initially

**Target Cash Cost: VND 0-30M/month** (only if Technical Lead paid by ResidentOne)

### 7.2 Phase 2 Strategy (Selective Scaling)

**Recommended Approach:**
1. **Hire Sales Team:**
   - 1-2 people, base + commission structure
   - Critical for scaling building acquisition

2. **Hire Onboarding Team:**
   - 1-2 people, full-time
   - Critical for customer success and retention

3. **Evaluate Development Needs:**
   - If AI Factory + part-time support sufficient → no additional hiring
   - If insufficient → hire 1-2 developers or leverage WEBASEGROUP full-time

**Target Cash Cost: VND 27-100M/month** (depending on development needs)

### 7.3 Phase 3 Strategy (Full Scale)

**Recommended Approach:**
1. **Hire Operations Manager:**
   - Critical for coordinating operations at scale
   - Can be equity + salary to attract experienced talent

2. **Expand Sales & Onboarding:**
   - Scale based on building acquisition targets

3. **Optional: Data Analyst**
