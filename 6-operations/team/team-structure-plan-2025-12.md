# Team Structure Plan — ResidentOne Platform

**Date:** 2025-12-13  
**Last Updated:** 2025-12-13  
**Status:** Comprehensive Team Structure Plan  
**Purpose:** Define detailed team structure with scope, job descriptions, and positions

---

## Executive Summary

This document provides a comprehensive team structure plan for ResidentOne platform, including:
- **Team Scope Definitions** - Clear scope and responsibilities for each team
- **Job Descriptions (JD)** - Detailed JDs for all positions
- **Organization Structure** - Reporting relationships and hierarchy
- **Position Definitions** - Specific roles, levels, and requirements

**Key Principles:**
- **AI-First Development** - Leverage AI Factory tools to minimize development costs
- **Leverage Existing Resources** - Utilize WEBASEGROUP and 8Agency teams
- **Equity-First Approach** - Minimize cash burn with equity-based compensation where possible
- **Scalable Structure** - Design for growth from 5-10 buildings (Phase 1) to 50-100 buildings (Phase 3)

## 3. Organization Structure

### 3.1 Phase 1: Foundation (Months 1-6)

**Organization Chart:**

```
Board / Co-Founders
├── Product Lead (Hưng) - Equity-only
│   ├── Technical Lead (Ngân) - WEBASEGROUP
│   │   ├── AI Factory (Development)
│   │   └── WEBASEGROUP Tech Team (Support)
│   └── Building Onboarding Specialist (Month 3-4)
│       └── Customer Support (Month 4-5, shared)
├── Finance & Strategy Lead (Yad) - Equity-only, 8Agency
└── Marketing Strategy Lead (Tuyền) - Equity-only, 8Agency
    └── 8Agency Marketing Team (Support)
```

**Key Characteristics:**
- Flat structure with Product Lead as primary coordinator
- Technical Lead reports to Product Lead
